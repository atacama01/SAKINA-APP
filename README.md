Sakina
is a simple Android application that helps you avoid forgetting your phone in silent mode.
The app allows you to set your phone to silent mode for a specific duration (e.g. during prayer, exams, meetings, studying, lectures, or sleep) and automatically restores the previous sound mode when the timer ends.

Features

* 🔇 One-tap silent mode timer
* ⏱️ Custom timer duration
* 🔔 Automatic sound restoration
* 📱 Simple and clean user interface
* 📢 Notification showing remaining time
* 💾 Works completely offline
* 🚫 No account required
* 🚫 No subscriptions
* 🚫 No ads

 Use Cases

Mosque & Prayer
Set your phone to silent mode before entering the mosque and let the app restore sound automatically afterward.

Exams
Silence your phone for the duration of an exam without worrying about forgetting to turn sound back on.

Meetings
Avoid interruptions during meetings while ensuring your phone returns to normal afterward.

Study Sessions
Stay focused without missing important calls later.

Sleep
Set silent mode before sleeping and automatically restore sound in the morning.

Technology Stack

* Flutter
* Riverpod
* Flutter Local Notifications
* Android Alarm Manager
* Shared Preferences

 Requirements

* Android 5.0 (API 21) or newer

Permissions
The app may request:

* Notification permission
* Do Not Disturb access

These permissions are required to manage silent mode and notify the user when the timer finishes.


Privacy
sakina does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The application works entirely on the device.
